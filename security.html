💡 Safety Tips & Best Practices

  • Be Aware: Always stay alert to your surroundings, especially in unfamiliar places.
  • Share Your Location: Keep a trusted friend or family member informed about your whereabouts.
  • Use Well-lit Routes: Avoid dark or isolated areas when walking alone at night.
  • Keep Emergency Numbers Handy: Save police and emergency contacts in your phone.
  • Trust Your Instincts: If something feels off, leave immediately and seek help.

🚨 What to Do If...

🔍 You Feel Followed

Cross the street, enter a busy place, or call someone to make your presence known. If needed, go to the nearest police station.

📢 Someone is Harassing You

Speak loudly to draw attention. If in a public space, approach a group for assistance. Report the incident to authorities.

🗺️ You’re Lost in an Unfamiliar Area

Find a safe, public place like a café or store. Use GPS or call someone for directions.

🚕 Your Ride Feels Unsafe

Share your trip details with someone. If you feel uneasy, ask the driver to stop at a well-populated area and exit.
